    After the success of the 2018 Hue Half Marathon, the 2019 Hue Half Marathon continues to be prepared with perfection and improvement over the previous season.


    1. Introduction to running solution:

    The pioneer of the Hue Half Marathon in 2019 this year is the Hue Half Marathon run 2018 - the first professional running run in Hue with thousands of participants. Because of the resounding successes, the 2019 run has attracted great attention from the jogging community, promising a useful playground, networking and community connection.
